Our Mission & Vision

Our Vision

To help build healthier, better-connected communities by reducing food insecurity, expanding charitable assistance, and encouraging community wellness — from Las Vegas to the world.

Our Mission

Vegas ALL Non-Profit Corporation serves the local Las Vegas community and, as resources permit, supports carefully supervised charitable activities internationally — uniting people through community wellness programs, outreach, and nourishment. We use accessible activities, including pickleball, to build community, raise funds, and support hunger relief and wellness initiatives.

Our Four Pillars of Impact

How a simple game creates lasting, positive waves across our local community.

01

FEEDING

Vegas All plans to operate hunger-relief initiatives providing nutritious food to vulnerable and underserved populations, locally in Las Vegas and, as resources permit, internationally. Our founders' and volunteers' independent food-distribution work in the Philippines in 2025 and 2026 helped inspire this pillar.

02

FUNDRAISING

Organizing high-energy, community-driven charity events that generate essential resources — supporting local nonprofits, international aid projects, and qualified charitable organizations whose activities further Vegas All's charitable and educational purposes.

03

WELLNESS & RECREATION

Championing health and active living through accessible community wellness programs — including pickleball, open play, and group activities — that build social connection and support stronger, healthier communities.

04

COMMUNITY OUTREACH

Vegas All plans to provide direct community outreach — connecting individuals and families with charitable assistance and community resources — extending our impact beyond the court and into the everyday needs of Las Vegas families.
