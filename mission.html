Our Mission & Vision

Our Vision

To build a globally connected, actively engaged, and well-nourished society — where the joy of sport directly fuels the eradication of hunger, bringing communities together for good.

Our Mission

Vegas ALL Non-Profit Corporation serves both the local Las Vegas community and international initiatives by uniting people through sports, outreach, and nourishment. We leverage the power of pickleball to combat hunger, strengthen mental wellness, and create meaningful community bonds.

Our Four Pillars of Impact

How a simple game creates lasting, positive waves across our local community.

01

FEEDING

Operating hunger relief initiatives that deliver nutritious meals to vulnerable and underserved populations — both locally in Las Vegas and internationally, including our 2025 Philippines Food Drive.

02

FUNDRAISING

Organizing high-energy, community-driven charity events that generate essential resources — supporting local nonprofits, international aid projects, and the causes closest to our hearts.

03

PICKLEBALL

Leveraging the inclusive, fast-growing sport of pickleball as our platform — building social connection, promoting active lifestyles, and turning play into purpose.

04

WELLNESS

Championing health and wellbeing as a foundation for stronger communities — because the same energy that drives a great match on the court is the energy we bring to serving others.
