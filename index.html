Our Vision

To build a globally connected, actively engaged, and well-nourished society — where the joy of sport directly fuels the eradication of hunger, bringing communities together from Las Vegas to the world.

Our Mission

Vegas ALL Non-Profit Corporation serves both the local Las Vegas community and international initiatives by uniting people through sports, outreach, and nourishment. We leverage the universal appeal of pickleball to raise funds and awareness, while operating direct programs that alleviate hunger and food insecurity — one paddle, one plate, one community at a time.

Our Four Pillars of Impact

How a simple game creates lasting, positive waves across our local community.

01

FEEDING

Operating hunger relief initiatives that deliver nutritious meals to vulnerable and underserved populations — both locally in Las Vegas and internationally, including our 2026 toy and food drive in the Philippines.

02

FUNDRAISING

Organizing high-energy, community-driven charity events that generate essential resources — supporting local nonprofits, international aid projects, and the causes closest to our members' hearts.

03

PICKLEBALL

Leveraging the inclusive, fast-growing sport of pickleball as our platform — building social connection, promoting active lifestyles, and turning play into purpose.

04

WELLNESS

Championing health and wellbeing as a foundation for stronger communities — because the same energy that drives a great match on the court is the energy we bring to serving others off of it.
