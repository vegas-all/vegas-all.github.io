🧸 Paddles & Playthings Toy Drive

Help us bring pure smiles and childhood wonder to underprivileged youth. We are collecting new, unwrapped toys at all open-play courts this month!

How You Can Help

🎁 Donate Toys

Drop off new, unwrapped toys at any of our open-play court locations. All toys go directly to children in need.

🏓 Volunteer

Help us organize, sort, and distribute toys. Your time and effort make a real difference!

💰 Donate Funds

Financial donations help us purchase toys for areas with the greatest need.
