About Vegas ALL

Vegas ALL began the way great movements often do — with a group of individuals brought together by a shared love of the game. What started as friendly pickleball matches in Las Vegas quickly evolved into something greater: a community rooted in connection, active living, and purpose.

As our members' bond deepened, so did our reach — local meetups became out-of-town trips, and out-of-town trips became international outreach. In 2026, Vegas ALL organized a toy and food drive in the Philippines, an experience that redefined who we are and what we're capable of as an organization.

Today, Vegas ALL Non-Profit Corporation stands at the intersection of sport and service. We leverage the universal, inclusive appeal of pickleball to build community and raise funds — channeling that energy directly into hunger relief, wellness initiatives, and outreach programs that serve vulnerable populations both at home and abroad.

We believe sport has the power to do more than bring people together on the court — it can nourish, connect, and uplift entire communities. This is Vegas ALL: serving up hope on the court, and fighting hunger in the community, from Las Vegas to the world.
