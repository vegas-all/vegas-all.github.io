About Vegas ALL

Vegas ALL began the way great movements often do — with a group of individuals brought together by a shared love of the game. What started as friendly pickleball matches in Las Vegas quickly evolved into something greater: a community rooted in connection, active living, and purpose.

As the bonds among our founders, volunteers, and supporters deepened, so did our reach — local meetups became out-of-town trips, and out-of-town trips became international outreach. Before Vegas All was formed, its founders and volunteers independently conducted food-distribution and charitable outreach activities in the Philippines in 2025 and 2026 — an experience that directly inspired the organization we've since built.

Today, Vegas ALL Non-Profit Corporation stands at the intersection of sport and service. We use the universal, inclusive appeal of pickleball to build community and raise funds — channeling that energy into hunger relief, wellness initiatives, and outreach programs that serve vulnerable populations, primarily in Las Vegas and, as resources permit, abroad.

We believe sport has the power to do more than bring people together on the court — it can nourish, connect, and uplift entire communities. This is Vegas ALL: serving up hope on the court, and fighting hunger in the community, from Las Vegas to the world.
